DEMON CONTRACT

You have made a contract with a poverfull demonical or fiendish creature, this contract grants them a piece of their power, but also takes somethingin return, choose one positive and one negative effect from below:

Positive effects:

Demonic skin: You gain immunity to fire damage

Demonic claws:Your nails become claws that can be used as a natural weapon dealing 1d8 + your Strength modifier damage

Demonic charm:Your body and face becomes more beautifull, you become more attractive and roll all persuaition, deception and performance checks with advantage



Negative effects:

Your immortal soul:if you fall unconcious with 0 hit points you automaticallly fail all 3 death saves

Your age:You become 50 years older

Your memories:You forget most details of what happened to you before the last week
